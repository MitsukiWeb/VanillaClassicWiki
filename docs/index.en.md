We are not just another empty "High Rate" server. We have integrated modern systems to keep the game **engaging, active, and rewarding**.

### Progress and Reward Systems

!!! success "Play and Earn"
    Forget about mindless farming. In Vanilla Classic, every action counts:
    
    * **Battle Pass:** Unlock exclusive rewards simply by playing and completing objectives.
    * **Time Points:** Earn special coins just for staying online.
    * **Daily Login:** Useful gifts every day you visit the server.

### Competitiveness and Honor

!!! quote "Glory to the brave"
    Prove you are the best with our ranking systems:
    
    * **Multiple Rankings:** Compete in various categories.
    * **Medals of Honor:** Obtain medals by ranking and exchange them for **valuable items**.
    * **Hunting System (Reputation):** Eliminate specific monsters, raise your reputation, and access unique benefits.

### Quality of Life (QoL)

We respect your time with features that don't break the game but make it more enjoyable:

* :hospital: **Healer in Cities:** Heal up and get basic buffs quickly.
* :tea: **Improved Rest:** **Accelerated HP and SP** recovery while sitting. Goodbye to waiting 10 minutes for mana!
* :tada: **Automated Events:** Constant fun without depending on a GM being online.
* :artist: **Customization NPC:** Customize your items and stand out visually.
